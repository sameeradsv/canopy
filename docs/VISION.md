# Vision

Canopy exists to reduce cognitive load caused by repeatedly processing:
- interactions,
- ambiguous signals,
- invisible responsibilities,
- recurring operational burdens,
- and emotionally expensive patterns.

The goal is not automation of human relationships.

The goal is contextual continuity.

---

# Problem Statement

Humans repeatedly:
- forget context,
- reinterpret recurring situations,
- emotionally recompute old interactions,
- and lose operational clarity over time.

Canopy acts as:
- contextual memory,
- lightweight behavioral analytics,
- and cognitive infrastructure.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Create a system that:
- preserves meaningful context,
- reduces emotional overprocessing,
- improves decision consistency,
- and augments human memory without becoming intrusive.

---

# Design Direction

The system should:
- remain local-first,
- minimize input friction,
- support uncertainty,
- and prioritize retrieval quality over exhaustive logging.

The system should feel:
- calm,
- sparse,
- reflective,
- and trustworthy.
